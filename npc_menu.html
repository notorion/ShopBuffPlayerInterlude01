Greetings, adventurer! For your convenience, I will be available every hour to apply a buff. For the price of 5,000,000 adenas.
To disable this feature, click "unFollow".
NPC Follow is available next to the player to grant special buffs with a duration of 5 seconds. By interacting with it, you will gain access to an exclusive menu. The NPC's reappearance can be disabled by clicking 'Unfollow,' logging out, or speaking to NPC Sonia, located near the city's Gatekeeper. NPC Follow will follow the player, even inside instances. Buff Follow provides support to the player in combat against monsters. If the player enters attack mode against another player, GrandBoss, RaidBoss, or gains Karma, the short-duration buffs will be immediately canceled. Only players who purchase the support of NPC Buff Follow can access the exclusive menu. To check real-time availability, select "Check Availability" in the menu.
